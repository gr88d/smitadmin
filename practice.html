HOSPITALITY AND MANAGEMENT

Course Overview

This course provides a comprehensive overview of the hospitality industry, exploring its key sectors including lodging, food and beverage, travel and tourism, and event management. Students will gain foundational knowledge in hospitality operations, customer service, leadership, and business practices specific to the industry. Emphasis is placed on developing professional skills, understanding global trends, and applying management principles to real-world hospitality settings. This course prepares students for further study and careers in a dynamic, service-focused field.
