Mission

Southern Mindanao Institute of Technology commits to provide the community with competent, ethical, and research-oriented graduates through:

  • Efficient and effective faculty and staff
  • Adequate and appropriate instructional facilities
  • Innovative and responsive teaching strategies

Vision

Southern Mindanao Institute of Technology, Inc is a learner-centered institution committed to quality education.

Core Values

SMIT is a time-conscious institution that embraces Service, Modesty, Integrity, and Trust.
